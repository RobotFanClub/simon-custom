About Me

I'm Simon Peng! I currently work in marketing, making brands (and many other things) for people and companies that want to speak to who they really are and what they actually believe.

I'm a teacher at heart. I taught graphic design and illustration at Mohawk College and now continue bringing an educator's perspective to all the work I do.

I believe that Everyone's Creative. My aim is to lift the veil on the creative process, to show people that there's really no such thing as "natural talent" and that artistic "geniuses" just had lots of opportunities and are good at hiding their mistakes.

Where to Find Me

You've found me! This site is my main blog and I share basically everything I do here. If you'd like to get in touch, you can send me an email at hello@simonpeng.net or find me on socials.
